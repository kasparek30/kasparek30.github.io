Prospects National Team - Trevor Sanford, Head of Player Development

We recruit top, amateur talent to compete in highly competitive tournaments across the country. Our goal is to give talented ballplayers the opportunity to play in front of Major League Baseball scouts and Collegiate recruiters. With the hopes that all of our players receive a college scholarship, and/or a phone call on draft day, we also work to prepare them for the next level. Our coaching staff has either played at the NCAA D1 level, all levels of the Minor Leagues, and/or the Major Leagues! Become part of our team and prepare to take the next step in getting to the Big Leagues!
